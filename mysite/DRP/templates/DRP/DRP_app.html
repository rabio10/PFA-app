DRP APP

DRP APP is a Django web application that allows you to make a demand forecast for a product.

It is based on the Double Exponential Smoothing method.

It is composed of 3 main parts:

  • Prevision: Make a forecast for a product
  • History: Display the history of the forecast for a product
  • Infos: Display the informations of the forecast for a product

Click on the links below to access the different parts of the application.

Enjoy!
